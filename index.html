Projects

Air-Ground Collaboration

An air-ground system for weak-GPS forest environments. I integrated the UAV hardware stack, built ROS 2 links between the UAV, UGV, and ground station, and used relative pose registration with soft time synchronization for shared localization and coordinated tasks.

ROS 2 Mobile Manipulator

A ROS 2 Mecanum mobile manipulation system with distributed modules for drive, localization, navigation, vision, and arm control. It combines STM32 communication, odometry-IMU fusion, AprilTag pose estimation, and arm IK for closed-loop grasping, with Gazebo validation under sensor noise and communication delay.

Wheeled Humanoid Robot

A wheeled humanoid platform for autonomous navigation and upper-limb manipulation, focused on deploying learned grasping policies on edge compute for stable real-robot execution.
